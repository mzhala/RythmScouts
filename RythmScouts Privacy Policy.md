# RythmScouts Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** November 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how RythmScouts ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our mobile application (the "App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information directly from you when you create an account, use the App's features, and interact with our service.

### A. Information You Directly Provide

When you register for and use the App, you may provide the following personal data:

- **Contact and Profile Data:** Your Email Address, Username, First Name, and Last Name (optional).  
- **Account Identifiers:** A unique User ID (UID) generated by Firebase Authentication is associated with your account.  
- **Profile Preference:** Your selected in-app profile picture (stored as a resource ID) or a photo URL (if using Google Sign-In).  
- **Content Data:** Any personal notes or comments you add to saved events.  

### B. Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we collect non-personal data related to your activity:

- **App Preferences:** Your settings choices, such as Dark Mode preference and selected Language preference.  
- **Usage Data:** Information about how you use the app, which helps us diagnose errors and improve features.  

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Account Management:** To create, maintain, and secure your account and allow you to log in (via Email/Password or Google Sign-In).  
- **Service Functionality:** To save and display your personalized list of events and venue details.  
- **Personalization:** To store your personal notes/comments on events and display your preferred profile picture and name in the settings.  
- **Communication:** To send you notifications about events you have saved (if you have enabled this feature and granted the necessary device permissions).  
- **App Improvement:** To monitor usage trends and improve the user experience, performance, and stability of the App.  

---

## 3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We disclose your information only in the following ways:

### A. Third-Party Service Providers

We use Google Firebase to provide core functionalities for the App. Your data is stored on and managed by these services:

- **Firebase Authentication:** Handles user registration, login, and secure user ID management.  
- **Firebase Realtime Database:** Stores your profile information (Username, Name, Email, Profile Image ID) and your saved event data (including comments).  

### B. Legal and Compliance

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights or property, or protect the safety of the public or other users.  

---

## 4. Data Security

We implement reasonable physical, technical, and administrative security measures designed to protect your personal information. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of our systems. You are responsible for keeping your password secure.  

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the services of the App. If you delete your account, we will delete your profile data and saved events from the Firebase Realtime Database.  

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information from our files as soon as possible.  

---

## 7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on our website. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
